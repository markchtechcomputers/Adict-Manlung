About

Adict Manlung is a rising rap artist and storyteller whose music captures the raw reality of the streets, the struggles of survival, and the journey of turning pain into power. Known as The Flock Storyteller, his verses blend personal experiences of loss, betrayal, and redemption with hard-hitting bars and unapologetic truth.

From the tough corners of everyday hustle to dreams of global recognition, Adict Manlung's artistry is built on authenticity. Every track is more than music—it's a diary, a testimony, and a message to the youth that no matter the struggle, legends are made through resilience.

With a unique sound that mixes gritty street rap and emotional depth, Adict Manlung is not just making songs; he's building a legacy.
